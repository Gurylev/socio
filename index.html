Why social buttons is bad?

Each social button...

  • allows soc. networks to monitor users
  • loads on the script and to frame the document
  • slows down the loading of your precious pages
  • blocked by AdBlock users
  • cause an error in the browser console
  • does not allow the document to pass W3C-validated

Get rid of social buttons!

Overwrite a social buttons to simple links. When you click on this link you will see the same result that after installing the official social buttons.
